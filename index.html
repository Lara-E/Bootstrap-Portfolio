About Me

Full Stack Web Developer with knowledge in JavaScript, JQuery, CSS, HTML, Node.js, Express, React, SQL, MongoDB, and Handlebars. I constantly strive to expand upon my knowledge and refine my skills and I love creating apps that are both intuitive and fun to use, while solving real world problems. Leveraging the people skills I learned through my many years of bartending, I always aim to create apps that are intuitive and user friendly. Whether working alone, or collaborating with others, I enjoy developing applications that look, feel, and act seamlessly. I genuinely strive to create programs that I can be, and am proud of.
